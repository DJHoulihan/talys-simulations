Gamma Strength Functions for 112,114,116,118,120,124Sn
------------------------------------------------------

Files starting f1.... total GSF
Files starting fe1... E1 part of GSF
Files starting fm1... M1 part of GSF

Total GSF is the sum of E1 and M1 parts determined separately

The experiments, data analysis and methods for the exctraction of E1 and M1 strengths
are described in
